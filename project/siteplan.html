Site Plan

Finance in Focus

Finance in Focus It's a clear and straightforward name that conveys the idea that the site addresses investments, financial organization, and economic education with seriousness and objectivity. It's a brand that inspires trust in both beginners and finance enthusiasts.

Disponibilidade de domínio (opcional): financeinfocus.com.br

Website Objective

The Website Finance in focus It aims to offer free content on personal finance and investments. The goal is to educate the public on how to save money, invest wisely, build an emergency fund, and achieve financial freedom. Useful tools such as calculators, simulators, and downloadable spreadsheets will also be available.

Scenarios:

  • How can I start investing even with little money?
  • What are the best investments for beginners in Brazil in 2025?

Color Schema

  • Money Green (#2E7D32): for titles, buttons, and important elements such as charts and positive alerts.
  • White (#FFFFFF): as the main background color and to ensure readability in the body of the content.

Typography

  • Títulos: Montserrat, sans-serif — for titles, menus, and buttons. Modern and impactful.
  • text body: Roboto, sans-serif — Readable and friendly for long texts.
